Milestone: Initial Setup
Epic: User Account Management
Feature: User Registration
User Story: As a new user, I want to create an account so that I can save my searches.
Acceptance Criteria:
1. Users can enter their email and password to register.
2. Users receive a confirmation email after registration.
3. Users can log in with their credentials after registration.
Issues to Address:
1. No validation for email format during registration.
2. Password strength requirements are not enforced.
3. No feedback provided to users if registration fails.
4. Confirmation email may not be sent if the email service is down.
